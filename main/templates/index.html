Prefecture in Focus: Tottori

Tottori Prefecture, nestled in western Japan, is a hidden gem awaiting discovery. Renowned for its vast sand dunes, Tottori offers a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural charm. Explore the Tottori Sand Dunes, the largest in Japan, where endless golden sands stretch to meet the sea.
